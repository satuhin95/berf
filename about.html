About Us
BERF is an non-profit, non-government, research, policy making, capacity building and implementation institution working on environment development at locally and globally. It was registered under societies act-1860 in 2012. Through the expertise of BERF, builds partnership with government, different stakeholders and international leaders to work collaborating to solve environment problem. BERF working in different capacities with a large youth group, senior professionals, scientists and researchers that are closely related to environment and sustainable development in the world.
